Heia! I'm a Dysgu Cymraeg Welsh learner, currently at level Uwch. I use technology extensively to support my learning by creating apps and lessons, which I am sharing here in case it's helpful to other learners. Here, you'll find a range of tools for learning key grammar and vocabulary as well as some "mini-lessons" on the basics. Hope it's useful and good luck on your language journey!
